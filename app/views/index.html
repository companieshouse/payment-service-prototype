Companies House Payment Service prototype

The prototype to guide the development of a user journey for the payment service.

Standard Service Journey

In this journey, the payment service is attached to a single Companies House service journey (the 'originating service'). Examples may include File company accounts, Pay a late filing penalty or Close a company.

In this scenario, the originating service would retain control of the user journey for reviewing what is being paid for and also for final confirmation of payment/submission.

{{ govukButton({ text: "Single Service Journey", href: "choose-payment-method" }) }}

3rd Party Application Journey

In this journey, the payment service is attached to a 3rd part application or service. Examples include retail software filing applications where bulk filings are supported.
